Every Saturday from 7 to 8:30pm we hold a free tournament that is open to all MIT students and club members. We encourage you to join us!!

Warm up Time

The first hour of every saturday (from 6 to 7pm) is allotted to warm up time and open practice (under the guidance of Coach Liang). The Saturday Tournament begins at 7pm.

Format

1) The tournament will initially consist of a round robin stage with 4 groups.
2) Each group will consist of 3-5 players (depending on turnout).
3) The group stage games will be a best of three games each.
4) The top two players from each group will advance to the single elimination stage.
5) Each single elimination match is a best of 5 games

Point System

Based on their performance, players will receive the following points each tournament:

Winner - 8 points
Runner up - 6 points
Semi finalist - 4 points
Quarter Finalist - 2 points
Participant - 1 point

Awards

Players will accumulate points over the course of the semester. At the end of the semester, the player with the most number of points will recieve a trophy. The top five ranked players will also recieve prizes.

If you would like to participate or want more information please contact us at the tabletennisclub@mit.edu
