2012 MIT Fall Open

The 2012 MIT Fall Open will be held on Sunday, October 21 at Dupont Court!

The fall open will feature 8 ratings events (Ratings Central):

  • Open Singles
  • U2100 Singles
  • U1800 Singales
  • U1500 Singles
  • U1200 Singles
  • Unrated Singles
  • Open Doubles
  • U3200 Doubles

All are welcome to participate!
