MIT Table Tennis Team Member Responsibilities

Once you become a member of the MIT Table Tennis team, you must fulfill the following responsibilities:

• Come to Practice: There are approximately 20 team practice sessions in the academic year. All team members are required to come to a minimum of 16. During the 2010 – 2011 academic year practices will be held every Wednesday from 9pm to 11pm in the T Club Lounge at the duPont Athletic Center (Building W32)

• Participate in Intercollegiate Competitions: All team members are required to participate in the NCTTA New England Regional tournaments held during October/November/December 2010 and January/February 2011. These tournaments are held on Saturdays and take up the whole day.

• Participate in Open Competitions: All team members are required to participate in at least one Open Competition such as the MIT Open or other USATT sanctioned tournaments. Participating in these competitions is an integral part of the members’ performance development. Note: It is not guaranteed that the club will fund such participation.

The team coach will prioritize team members that fulfill these responsibilities and will favor their participation in the NCTTA New England Regional Tournaments over noncompliant team members.

Women’s Team

The MIT Table Tennis Team is a coed team. However, if 4 or more women players, who are club members, decide to form a Women’s team, then it is at the coach’s discretion to organize its ranking and participation during team practice. Women can play on both the coed team and the Women’s team. During intercollegiate tournaments the Women's team competes only against other women's teams from other colleges.
