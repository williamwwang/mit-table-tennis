The MIT Table Tennis Club is open to both competitive and recreational players of all skill levels within the MIT community. For the Spring 2022 semester, as of February 8, 2022, our open practices are on Tuesdays and Fridays, and our team practices are on Saturdays. A full schedule is available here. Practices are held at the T-Club lounge in the du Pont Gymnasium (W32).

We are part of the National Collegiate Table Tennis Association, and we compete in their tournaments. We also periodically hold tournaments open to the general public. Additionally some of our members compete in other local tournaments.
