Welcome to the MIT Table Tennis website! Our club is open to all members of the MIT community, from recreational players to experienced competitors. We hope to provide a fun and accessible environment for everyone, and we encourage individuals of any skill level to join.

Club practices are held in the T-Club Lounge, which is located on the second floor of the duPont Athletic Center (W32). For the Fall 2023 semester, practices currently take place on Tuesdays, Fridays, and Sundays; a full schedule is available here.

We are part of the National Collegiate Table Tennis Association (NCTTA), and our team members compete regularly in multiple tournaments each season. In past years, MIT has established itself as a top team in our division, and we always look forward to welcoming new members.

For more information on how to join, check out our Contact page!
