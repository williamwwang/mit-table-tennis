The Table Tennis Club is part of the MIT Club Sports Program. As a result, membership is open to all MIT students, faculty, staff, alumni with a valid DAPER membership. Our goal is to foster a welcoming community for players of all levels and backgrounds.

The club offers members several quality tables that are in good condition. In addition, a large quantity of balls and a few very basic rackets are available for use during practices, although we highly encourage members to bring their own rackets.

For our Summer 2024 schedule, the club will have open-play practices three times a week. We encourage members to take full advantage of these sessions!

In addition to recreational play, our club also hosts competitive teams that participate in intercollegiate NCTTA tournaments. MIT has done very well at the regional and national level in recent years. During the 2021-22 season, our co-ed team won 2nd place at Divisionals, advancing to Regionals. The women's team won Regionals and eventually finished 3rd at Nationals.
