MIT Table Tennis Open 2025

After an 8-year hiatus, we are so excited to announce the return of MIT Table Tennis Open with our 2025 competition! Compete for cash prizes, gear, and bragging rights against fellow MIT players and local ping pong enthusiasts. All participants are welcome — MIT affiliation is not required.

Location: MIT Zesiger Sports and Fitness Center, 120 Vassar St, Cambridge, MA.

Tell the front desk you're here for the table tennis tournament before following the signs to Du Pont Court 2. The Du Pont courts can be a bit tricky to find, so don't be afraid to ask for directions!

Date: Saturday, May 3rd, 2025. See event times and details below.

Equipment: We'll be using Joola 3* Prime balls and 8 competition-sanctioned tables:

  • 6x Joola Tour 2500
  • 1x Joola SC Pro 3000
  • 1x Joola Rally Indoor 15mm
Please bring your own racket, though we will have a few spares available if needed!

Event Info:

  • Amateur
    No existing USATT / NCTTA rating, or a USATT / NCTTA rating < 1000
    10:30 AM | $10 entry | $40 prize | First to 11, best of 3 sets | 32 players max

    This event is designed for players who have mostly played casually and haven't competed before. If you’ve had formal training or competed seriously (even without a USATT rating), we encourage you to check out our rated events instead!

    If you aren't sure which events to play, feel free to email us at tt-officers@mit.edu and ask!
  • Open
    No rating cap
    12:30 PM | $30 entry | $100 prize | First to 11, best of 5 sets | 16 players max
  • U1900
    USATT rating < 1900
    1:30 PM | $25 entry | $80 prize | First to 11, best of 5 sets | 16 players max
  • U1500
    USATT rating < 1500
    3:00 PM | $20 entry | $60 prize | First to 11, best of 5 sets | 16 players max

Each event consists of 4-player round robins, with the top 2 from each group advancing to single elimination.

Payment: Please send us your total registration fee via either:

  • Venmo: @soph13w
  • Zelle: wu.sophie@live.com
Payments should be made at the time of submitting the entry form. Make sure to include your full name in the payment description! We will not be able to confirm your registration until your payment is received.

Spots are limited — sign up early to secure yours! We hope to see you there!
